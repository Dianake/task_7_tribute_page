What types of ice skating are there?

On this page we will find out about:

  • Figure skating
  • Hockey skating
  • Freestyle skating
  • Nordic skating


Here's some information on all four types of ice skating mentioned above:

  • Figure skaters use figure skates, have several different disciplines, and compete at the Olympics. You can find out more about figure skating on this Rookie road page.
  • Hockey players usually play in teams, and use hockey skates that look quite different from figure skates. There is some more information about hockey on this Twinkl page.
  • Freestyle skaters use all types of ice skates and create their own moves that range from footwork, to ground moves, to jumps. Read more about this interesting new style on this Mpora page.
  • Nordic skaters use very long blades on their skating boots and skate outdoors on frozen lakes and rivers. Find out more on the Xskating site.
